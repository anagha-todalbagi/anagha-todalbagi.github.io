# Brand — the Axiom System

Source of truth: [brand-guide.html](brand-guide.html) (Brand Guidelines v1.0, 2026). This file is the working summary; when in doubt, open the guide. The previous brand (warm-gray minimalism, Inter, the `a→t` arrow) is in git history — this system replaces it entirely.

## Positioning

A product leader who turns first-principles thinking into systems that compound — building AI-native products that scale, and teams that include.

Speaks to: Series A–C startup founders · executive headhunters & boards · future mentees & rising ICs.

Personality: **confident** (states the finding, then the proof — authority from being right, not loud), **inspiring** (shows what scale looks like, then hands over the map), **inclusive** (first principles apply to who's in the work, not just the work).

Lean in: AI-native · expert at scale · impact · evidence · refined · elegant.
Avoid: empty words · loud · overt hype · vague statements.

## The monogram

**A over T, divided by a single rule** — the stacked initials read as a first-principles fraction: two givens and the line that relates them.

- The mulberry rule is the only color in the mark; everything else is ink or paper.
- 1:1 square lockup · rule ≈ 2px at UI sizes · minimum height 24px · clear space = cap height.
- Variations: stacked (primary), reversed (on ink), inline `A – T` (favicon contexts), accent field.
- Don't: set the name in a sans, recolor the rule anything but mulberry/paper, stretch, outline, or place on a busy photo.

The wordmark is "Anagha Todalbagi" in Instrument Serif, tracking near 0.

## Voice

Spare, clear, reasoned. Remove the noise until only the truth is left — then make room for the reader in it.

1. **Evidence-led** — open with the finding and the number. Claims carry proof; adjectives don't.
2. **Unhurried** — short sentences. Room to think. Confidence sounds calm.
3. **Inclusive** — plain language, no gatekeeping jargon; explain the why.

We say: *"We cut onboarding from 9 steps to 3. Activation rose 40 points."*
We don't say: *"revolutionizing the future of AI-driven synergy"*, *"so excited to announce, humbled and thrilled…"*
