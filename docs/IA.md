# Information Architecture — anagha-todalbagi.github.io

## Format

Single page, one column, five numbered sections plus hero and footer. No nav bar — a small fixed mono "CONTACT" link top-right. The takeaway is available at every glance:

- **Glance 1 (0–10 s):** monogram + name + "Product Leader · Deep Tech at Scale · AI-Native" + the positioning lede + three stats. Who she is, with numbers.
- **Glance 2 (one scroll):** how she works, then shipped things with proof links. Why to believe her.
- **Glance 3:** the career ledger — Suki (seed, 5th hire) → Duetto → Commure → Microsoft. The arc.
- Everything after is texture: her on stage, who she is off stage, how to reach her.

## Cross-cutting

- Every claim resolves to a link, number, date, or named program.
